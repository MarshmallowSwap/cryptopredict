24h discussion period
The community debates the proposal in the governance forum before voting opens. This prevents rushed decisions and allows all voices to be heard.
3
48h voting window
CPRED holders vote YES, NO, or ABSTAIN. Staked CPRED counts as 2× voting power to reward long-term holders. Minimum 51% quorum required.
4

Passed proposals enter a 24h timelock before on-chain execution. This gives users time to exit positions if they disagree with major changes.
