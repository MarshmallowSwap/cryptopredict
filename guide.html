How to Use Trading

Analyze markets with the on-chain price chart and place limit orders that execute automatically when the price reaches your target.

⏱ ~5 minutes · Difficulty: Medium
STEP 01
Select market and read chart
Go to /trading.html and select a market from the dropdown. The chart loads by reading BetPlaced events directly from Base Sepolia blockchain. Each point = the implied YES probability (0→1) at that bet. Green = YES bet, red = NO bet.
💡 Rising chart = more people betting YES

STEP 02
Place a limit order
Choose YES or NO, select Limit, set the maximum price you're willing to pay (0–1) and the amount. The order is saved on Supabase. The backend executes it automatically when the on-chain price reaches your limit. Example: YES at max 0.40 = buys when market thinks YES ≤ 40%.
