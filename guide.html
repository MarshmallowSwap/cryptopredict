How to Use Trading

Analyze markets with the blockchain price chart and place automatic limit orders

⏱ ~3 minutes
Difficulty: Medium
01
Select the market
Go to /trading.html e scegli un mercato dal dropdown in alto. The chart loads automatically reading BetPlaced from the Base Sepolia blockchain — no intermediate server.
💡 Il grafico mostra la probabilità implicita YES (0–1) ad ogni scommessa

02
Read the chart
Each point is a bet. Verde = YES, rosso = NO. The Y axis goes from 0% to 100% — shows the market's implied probability the event will occur. A rising chart means more people are betting YES.

03
Place a limit order
Choose YES or NO, select "Limit", set the maximum price you're willing to pay (0–1) and the amount. The order is saved on Supabase. The backend will automatically execute it when the on-chain price reaches your limit.
💡 Example: "I want YES at max 0.40" — buys when the market thinks YES at 40% or less
