02 · MARKETS
How Markets Work
Every market is a binary question with a YES or NO answer and an expiry date. Users bet in the market's native currency (chosen by the creator). At expiry the market is resolved and winners claim their proportional payout.
Lifecycle
1
Creation
The creator deposits initial liquidity (min 0.0025 ETH or equivalent), selects currency, category and expiry date. Requires 1,000 CPRED in wallet or staking.
2
Open Bets
Users place YES or NO bets in the market's currency. Each bet enters the corresponding pool and generates yield. The implied price adjusts based on flows.
3
Expiry
At the set date the market stops accepting bets. The team/resolver analyzes the result and resolves the market on-chain.
4
Resolution & Payout
Winners call claimPayout() and receive their share of the total pool (including accrued yield) net of protocol fees (2%).

03 · POOLS
Pools & Liquidity
Every market has two pools: YES pool and NO pool. Bets enter the corresponding pool. The creator funds the initial liquidity to avoid a zero pool.
How the pool works
Total pool = YES pool + NO pool + yield accrued
% YES = YES pool / Total pool × 100
% NO = NO pool / Total pool × 100
The creator's initial liquidity is placed on the YES side. This ensures the market starts with an implied price of 100% YES and adjusts as NO bets arrive. Capital generates automatic yield at 4.8% APY while waiting for resolution. Initial liquidity is fully refunded at market close.

06 · PAYOUT
Payout Calculation
Payout is proportional to the share of the winning pool. Those who bet more earn more, but those who bet on a less popular side have higher potential payout.
Gross payout = (Total pool + Yield) × (Your bet / Winning side pool)
Net payout = Gross payout × 0.98 (after 2% fee)

Example:
Pool YES: 0.0075 ETH · Pool NO: 0.0025 ETH · Your bet YES: 0.001 ETH
Gross = 0.01 × (0.001 / 0.0075) = 0.001333 ETH
Net = 0.001333 × 0.98 = 0.001307 ETH (+30.7%)
Payout is available as soon as the market is resolved. There is no withdrawal deadline. claimPayout(marketId) is callable directly or via the "Claim Winnings" button in Portfolio.

Dual Reward

2. CPRED (fee share) — 2% of every market payout is collected in the market's native currency (ETH, USDC, USDT or CPRED). 16 · TRADING
Trading & Price Chart
The page /trading.html offers advanced analysis and trading tools based on on-chain data and an off-chain orderbook via Supabase.
Implied Price Chart
The chart reads BetPlaced events directly from the blockchain and reconstructs the implied YES price (0–1) per bet. Green points = YES bets, red = NO bets.
Price YES = YES pool / (YES pool + NO pool)
Price NO = 1 - Price YES

Example: YES pool = 75 ETH, NO pool = 25 ETH
→ Price YES = 0.75 (75% probability it happens)
→ Price NO = 0.25

18 · AMM
AMM Liquidity — Mercato Predittivo Live
Il problema che l'AMM risolve
Nel modello parimutuel attuale, chi scommette YES o NO deve aspettare la risoluzione del mercato (anche mesi) per incassare o perdere. Non può uscire prima. Se il mercato si muove a suo favore nel frattempo, non può monetizzare. I pool di liquidità AMM eliminano questo limite: trasformano ogni mercato predittivo in uno strumento tradabile in tempo reale.
Come funziona
Un Liquidity Provider (LP) deposita la valuta nativa del mercato nel pool. Il contratto crea automaticamente riserve YES e NO (split 50/50). I trader possono ora comprare e vendere YES/NO shares in qualsiasi momento a un prezzo determinato dalla curva CPMM (x×y=k). Ogni swap paga 0.3% di fee — di cui l'80% va all'LP, il 20% al treasury. L'LP è completamente neutrale sull'esito: guadagna dal volume di trading indipendentemente da chi vince.
💡 In una frase
Senza AMM: scommesse chiuse, capitale bloccato, nessun prezzo live. Con AMM: mercato liquido, uscita in qualsiasi momento, prezzo che riflette le probabilità in tempo reale. I pool di liquidità sono il motore che trasforma CryptoPredict da piattaforma di scommesse a mercato predittivo vero.
Chiunque può depositare la valuta nativa del mercato (ETH, USDC, USDT o CPRED) in qualsiasi pool aperto. Il protocollo divide il deposito automaticamente 50/50 in YES e NO. L'LP riceve LP token ERC-20 proporzionali alla sua quota e guadagna fees da ogni swap — senza mai prendere posizione sull'esito.
