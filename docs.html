01🔮 1. Overview
CryptoPredict is an on-chain prediction market on Base Sepolia. Users bet on future events (crypto, macro, sport, politica) usando ETH, USDC, USDT o CPRED. Capital in pools doesn't sit idle — generates automatic yield at 4.8% APY che viene distributed to winners and CPRED stakers.
What makes CryptoPredict different
Unlike Polymarket (USDC only, no yield), CryptoPredict supports 4 native currencies, generates automatic yield on every pool, distributes protocol fees to CPRED stakers, e allows anyone to create markets as long as they hold 1,000 CPRED.
02📊 2. How Markets Work
Every market is a binary question with a YES or NO answer and an expiry date. Users bet in the market's native currency (chosen by the creator). At expiry, the market is resolved and winners claim their proportional payout.
Lifecycle
1
Creation
The creator deposits initial liquidity (min 0.0025 ETH or equivalent), selects the currency, category and expiry date. Requires 1,000 CPRED in wallet or staking.
2
Open Bets
Users place YES or NO bets in the market's currency. Each bet enters the corresponding pool and generates yield. The implied price (% YES / % NO) adjusts based on flows.
3
Expiry
At the set date the market stops accepting bets. The team/resolver analyzes the result and resolves the market on-chain.
4
Resolution and payout
Winners call claimPayout() e receive their share of the total pool (including accrued yield) net of protocol fees (2%).

03💧 3. Pools & Liquidity
Every market has two pools: YES pool e NO pool. Bets enter the corresponding pool alla direzione scelta. The creator funds the initial liquidity to avoid having a zero pool.
How the pool works
Total pool = YES pool + NO pool + yield accrued
% YES = YES pool / Total pool × 100
% NO = NO pool / Total pool × 100
The creator's initial liquidity is distributed on the YES side of the pool. This ensures the market starts with an implied price of 100% YES and adjusts as NO bets arrive.
I capitali nel pool non rimangono fermi — generates automatic yield at 4.8% APY (vedi sezione Yield). The creator's initial liquidity is fully refunded at market close.
04📈 4. Automatic Yield on Pools
Capital in the pools doesn't sit idle
Ogni importo scommesso su un mercato genera yield automatico al 4.8% APY mentre attende la risoluzione. This yield is added to the total pool and increases the final payout for winners.
Da dove viene il 4.8% APY
Testnet vs Mainnet
On testnet the yield is simulated by the backend. On mainnet the yield will be real, generated by deploying capital on DeFi protocols like Aave.
1
Testnet (current) — Yield simulated by backend
The Python backend runs a cron every 24h che calcola il 4.8% APY sul capitale nei pool e scrive yieldAccrued nel contratto. It's a simulated number — no real ETH moves. Used to test payout mechanics with yield included.
2
Mainnet (roadmap v3) — Real yield on Aave / Compound
On Base mainnet, pool capital will be automatically deployed to Aave v3 o Compound while awaiting market resolution. The real yield (tipicamente 3-8% APY sulle stablecoin) is accumulated and added to the pool. At resolution, capital + yield is withdrawn from Aave and distributed to winners.
3
Protocol fee on-chain (already active now)
L'1% di protocol fee raccolta su ogni claimPayout() is immediately sent to the CPRED token ETH reward pool via depositRewards(). Questo è già funzionante on-chain sia su testnet che sarà su mainnet — is not simulated.

06🎯 6. Payout Calculation
Payout is proportional to the share of the winning pool. Those who bet more earn more, ma those who bet on a less popular side have higher potential payout.
Gross payout = (Total pool + Yield) × (Your bet / Winning side pool)
Net payout = Gross payout × 0.98 (after 2% fee)

Example:
Pool YES: 0.0075 ETH · Pool NO: 0.0025 ETH · Your bet YES: 0.001 ETH
Gross = 0.01 × (0.001 / 0.0075) = 0.001333 ETH
Net = 0.001333 × 0.98 = 0.001307 ETH (+30.7%)
When to claim
Payout is available as soon as the market is resolved. There is no withdrawal deadline. La funzione claimPayout(marketId) è chiamabile directly or via the button "Ritira Vincita" in Portfolio or in My Bets section.

16📉 16. Trading & Price Chart
The page /trading.html offers advanced analysis and trading tools based on on-chain data and an off-chain orderbook via Supabase.
Implied Price Chart
The chart reads BetPlaced directly from the blockchain e reconstructs the implied YES price (0–1) per bet. Each point represents how much the market "believes the event will happen.
Price YES = YES pool / (YES pool + NO pool)
Price NO = 1 - Price YES

Example: YES pool = 75 ETH, NO pool = 25 ETH
→ Price YES = 0.75 (75% probability it happens)
→ Price NO = 0.25
I punti sul grafico sono colorati: verde = scommessa YES, rosso = scommessa NO. The chart updates with every new on-chain bet.
Limit Order Orderbook
Limit orders are saved on Supabase and don't lock on-chain funds. When the backend detects the on-chain price has reached your limit, automatically executes the bet via placeBet().

18🔄 18. AMM — Automated Market Maker (Roadmap)
The AMM is the advanced version of trading on CryptoPredict — continuous prices, instant swaps, automatic liquidity. It's on the v2.0 roadmap and requires a new dedicated contract.
Difference between current model and AMM
Attuale (Parimutuel)AMM (v2.0)
EntryFix YES or NO, wait for resolutionBuy/sell shares at any time
PriceDetermined by pool at the timeContinuous curve x×y=k
ExitOnly at market resolutionSell whenever, market price
LiquidityUser betsLPs deposit and earn swap fees
SlippageNone (pool unico)Depends on available liquidity
How the AMM will work
1
Liquidity Provider (LP)
LPs deposit funds into the market's AMM pool. In exchange they receive LP tokens e earn swap fees (0.3%) on every trade. Liquidity is withdrawn at resolution.
2
Trader
Traders buy and sell YES/NO shares like on a DEX. Price moves along the CPMM curve (x×y=k). They can enter and exit at any time without waiting for resolution.
3
Risoluzione
At resolution winning shares are worth 1 (100% del pool) e losing shares 0. LPs withdraw their share of liquidity + accumulated fees.
Why it's on the roadmap and not now
The AMM requires a completely new contract (not modifiable on the current PredictionMarket), a specific audit for the price curve, e a LP token system. It's a fundamental but complex feature — current priority is consolidating the parimutuel model and the secondary market.
