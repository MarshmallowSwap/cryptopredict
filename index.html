HOW IT WORKS
Prediction Market.
DeFi Native.

CryptoPredict is a decentralized platform where you can buy and sell shares on the outcome of crypto events. A share's price reflects the market's perceived probability.

🔮
STEP 01
Choose an Event

Choose from active markets: from "BTC > $150k" to "Next DeFi hack", from DAO governance to token launches.

Each market has a YES or NO question with a precise expiry date.
⚡
STEP 02
Buy Shares

Shares are priced between $0.01 and $0.99. If you think the event will happen, buy "YES". Otherwise buy "NO".

Every share you buy at 65¢ is worth $1.00 if you're right. You can sell at any time before expiry.
💰
STEP 03
Collect the Profit

If the event happens as predicted, every share is worth $1.00 in USDC. Settlement is automatic via oracle.

Your capital also generates yield in USDC while waiting for resolution. Zero idle capital.
